Cómo lo hacemos / How We Do It

THE MONEY PART

Leagues and tournaments generate the revenue. That revenue funds free programming, the health clinic, and the mutual aid fund. No grants required at launch.

Total annual league revenue (Phase 1) $49,760
Non-Negotiable / No Negociable

FREE IS THE POINT.

The leagues cross-subsidize everything free. Revenue from competitive play funds open access for everyone — no registration, no fee, no barrier.

Las ligas financian lo gratuito. Ese es el modelo.

THE FUND
IS THE WHY.

Most organizations treat mutual aid as a nice-to-have. We built it into the structure from day one.

The community fund isn't what we do with profit. It's why we create it. Housed in the nonprofit arm. Protected by design. Governed by the people it serves.

El fondo no es lo que hacemos con las ganancias. Es la razón por la que las creamos.
